About Me

Hi! I'm a senior at the California Institute of Technology studying computer science. I did research for two summers at the NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL), and also completed an internship at Rubrik.

As an undergraduate, I have become passionate about systems and databases research. Today's data infrastructure and globally-available apps are putting new requirements on system scalability and availability. Next-generation systems are being built using interesting algorithms and techniques such as distributed consensus and elastic data repartitioning. But much more work still needs to be done in the scalability of ML for big data, data discovery to help enterprises navigate their data, and availability to make apps more reliable than before. Through working at Rubrik and doing systems research, I hope to be at the forefront of this.

With a passion for technology and a well-rounded and rigorous education from Caltech, here is what I can contribute:

  • Strong foundation in computer science, including data structures and algorithms
  • Experience developing software in teams
  • Experience developing large systems from my internship at Rubrik
  • Fast learner, enjoying a fast-paced environment
  • Enjoy solving technical challenges
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills
  • Solid foundation in math, statistics, and science
